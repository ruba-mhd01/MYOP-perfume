WHAT WE DO

Make Your Own Perfume, MYOP is India's first perfume bar known for
high-quality, long-lasting fragrances with unparalleled expertise in the art
and science of perfumery. MYOP perfumes, reformulated with
50% fragrance oil concentration last longer in tropical weather
conditions. Our experts can guide the customer to not just select the right
perfume, but also provide them with the unique experience of mixing
perfumes to create a fully personalised olfactory experience.
